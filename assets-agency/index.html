About

Curious to learn more about us? Dive deeper and discover how we can help bring your best work to life! Click here to explore our publishing services, view our impressive catalog of published books, and meet the professional team ready to support you at every stage of the publishing process. Don’t hesitate to reach out if you have any questions or need more information. We’re excited to share this creative journey with you!

Chars Publisher was founded by Charlos Pardomuan Purba, a fifth-semester university student with a passion for storytelling. While still balancing his studies, Charlos took a bold step into the publishing world, fueled by his desire to bring unique stories to life. His early success came from publishing two fiction novels, which gained significant attention and marked the beginning of his entrepreneurial journey.

Despite starting small, Charlos’s dedication quickly turned Chars Publisher into a growing business with multiple ongoing projects. His ability to connect with aspiring authors and deliver high-quality publications has positioned Chars Publisher as a promising player in the literary world, all while Charlos continues to pursue his education.
Over the past three years, Chars Publisher has achieved remarkable growth and success. We have proudly published over 50 fiction titles, showcasing a wide range of talented authors and captivating stories. Our dedication to quality and creativity has led to the sale of more than 20,000 copies, establishing Chars Publisher as a trusted name in the literary world. Each milestone reflects our commitment to supporting authors and bringing their visions to life, while continuously expanding our reach and impact.
